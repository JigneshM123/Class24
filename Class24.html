About Codeyoung


Codeyoung is a platform where your kids can let their imaginations run wild. Students are taught to be creative, innovative and use coding in the most unusual way possible.

Founded by IIT-Delhi alumni Shailendra Dhakad and Rupika Taneja, Codeyoung is on a mission to develop cognitive skills and creative thinking among children through coding.


Our Values


MISSION: Our mission is to provide effective and simplified education to empower our students to be the creators of tomorrow.


VISION: Our vision is to enhance the creativity, curiosity and confidence of every child in the world so they become confident individuals who can change the world in the future.
